Reservation agreement

Reservation agreement means an agreement relating to the future sale of a real estate (apartment and parking space) which is binding for the seller and grants purchaser the right to conclude sale and purchase agreement within the agreed time period once he paid the deposit.

The Parties may agree otherwise, where canceling the agreement trigers penalties for both parties.
